## Rewrite Briefs for Flagged Variants

**Variant 2 — rewrite instruction:**
Drop the "From ₹3 Cr" opener entirely. The price belongs in the lead form, not the headline. The financial insight in the body ("tends to look different in three years") is the right instinct but needs grounding — either remove the time horizon or replace it with a factual market signal from the brief (metro connectivity, rate environment) without inventing a number. Suggested headline direction: lead with the Thaltej address and the amenity standard, let the price surface as a qualifier in the form.

**Variant 4 — rewrite instruction:**
Preserve the body — "The address is the kind Ahmedabad families hold for a generation" is the strongest legacy line in this batch and should survive the rewrite. Rebuild the headline around one concrete reframe: the club amenity as a life position, the Thaltej address as a generational decision, or the contrast between what this configuration costs and what it delivers. Do not open with a bedroom count. Do not create a two-noun spec list.
