## What Pikorua Is

Pikorua Realty is a luxury real estate consultancy founded in 2021, headquartered in Ahmedabad. It operates as a trusted advisory firm for high-net-worth buyers — not a developer, not a listing portal. The model is curated, relationship-driven, and client-first.

The name comes from the Māori symbol *pikorua* — a double twist representing eternal trust, lifelong connections, and two paths joining. This is not incidental branding; it is the philosophical backbone of the firm.

**Primary tagline:** Trusted by Elites, NRIs & Celebrities
**Secondary tagline:** Good People, Great Properties
**Website headline:** City's Most Trusted Advisory for Luxury Properties
