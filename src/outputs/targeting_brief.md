### 2. Demographics
*   **Age:** 38–52 years. (Captures the established entrepreneur/senior professional at the peak of career).
*   **Household Income:** Top 5% of household income (Meta setting).
*   **Gender:** All. *Rationale:* Real estate at this tier is a "Family Committee" decision; suppressing gender will increase CPMs unnecessarily.
*   **NRI Segment:** **Yes.** Target via Google Ads and LinkedIn in Tier-1 Financial Hubs: Dubai (UAE), London (UK), Singapore, and New York/San Francisco (USA). Use English language settings; emphasize "Asset Provenance" and "Trust" as the primary value prop to bridge the distance gap.

---

### 3. Professional Targeting
*Targeting logic: LinkedIn (Professional) and Meta (Interest/Employer).*

*   **Job Titles:** CEO, Managing Director, Founder, Promoter, Partner, CFO, CMO, President, Executive Director.
*   **Industries:** Private Equity & Venture Capital, Investment Banking, Pharmaceuticals, Real Estate Development, Textile/Manufacturing, Logistics/Supply Chain, Management Consulting.
*   **Seniority:** C-Suite, VP, Partner, Owner/Founder.
*   **Platforms:**
    *   **LinkedIn:** Focus on "Job Seniority" and "Company Size" (500+ employees).
    *   **Meta:** Use "Employer" interest categories (e.g., "Business Owners," "Small Business Owners" - *Note: Meta's categorization of 'Business Owner' is broad, refine with 'Interests' in finance/wealth*).

### 4. Meta Ads Targeting Segments

*   **Interests:** Luxury real estate, Property investment, Wealth management, Golf, Business class travel, Private equity, Angel investor, Interior design (High-end).
*   **Behaviours:** Frequent international travellers, Business owners, High-value purchases (Luxury goods), Small business owners.
